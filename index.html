1.BUSINESS TYCOON

Being organized for the First time in Thapar University, Business Tycoon is an event to bring out the entrepreneurship skills in students. This event allows the students to use the campus and make a specific amount of money into something valuable. Teams have to earn money by making a specific product(s) and then selling it.

If you have an idea, we have the money to support it.
The teams will use a starting amount of Rs. 200 and 6hours to multiply the amount.
In the end, the profit earned will be added to the prize money.
So basically, it is like playing on a NO LIMITS table. So you never know….

3.BIZ PLAN

MAIN EVENTS-

1.TAARE ZAMEEN PAR- It is an event in which specially abled children, from various schools in Patiala and surrounding areas will be performing different activities like group dance, skits, singing, mime act, mehandi, and much more.

2.PLANETARIUM DOME- The event comprises of an inflatable and portable dome theater in which movies would be shown using attractive technology, providing knowledge in a way which is both uniquely entertaining and a tantalizing visual treat. The dome theater shows will offer a thrilling and visually immersive environment to its viewers which will breathe a new life in how they perceive the wonders of the nature. The movies to be played in the theater are highly informative and will help the students to delve into the dynamics of nature. The description about some of the movies is given below : The Ultimate Universe: From the edge of space and time, through vast fields of wondrous galaxies, to the majesty of our solar system, this show will take audiences on a grand tour of the universe and let them experience its most provocative secrets.It is a three dimensional journey from the edge of the universe through space and time to reach our home planet, witnessing the major components of the cosmos along the way. Black Holes: It takes you on a journey through one of the most mystifying, awe-inspiring phenomena in the universe: a black hole. Where do they come from? Where do they go? How do we find them? Is there one on Earth's horizon? Using the latest in full-dome,3D animation visualization technology.

3.DRDO- Defence Research & Development Organisation (DRDO) .DRDO dedicatedly working towards enhancing self-reliance in Defence Systems and undertakes design & development leading to production of world class weapon systems and equipment. In Thapar University they will be exhibiting their latest weapon technologies and developments.

4.NDRF- The National Disaster Response Force (NDRF), established under the DM Act, 2005, is a specialist force which is gradually emerging as the most visible and vibrant multi-disciplinary, multi-skilled, high-tech force of the NDMA capable of dealing with all types of natural and man-made disasters. In Thapar University, they will be demonstrating a rescue operation via a helicopter.

5.GREEN TECH- Philosphy Green tech solutions is a programme, initiated by the students of Thapar University aimed at alleviating the social,economic and environmental crisis facing the rural areas of Punjab by equipping it with the best technology from around the world. Dr.APJ Abdul Kalam,scientist and Former President of India , in his much acclaimed book ‘India 2020-A vision for the new millenium’ identified technological development and self reliance as ‘integral factors ‘in realizing India’s dreams of becoming a developed country.These ‘integral factors’ also constitute the guiding philosophy of Green Tech solutions .We believe that for every disease plaguing the roots of Punjab,there is a technical solution possible and that solution,if implemented carefully with much delibration, will without exception,end up being the best solution. This programme aims to find those solutions,develop them further and demonstrate their applicability in a real time environment.

6.HUG THE TREE- To promote the GO GREEN theme of ARANYA’12, mascots dressed in tree outfit would be roaming around the campus.

The following workshops will be organized during Aranya'12 -



1. ACCELERO-BOTIX


( Hand Gesture Controlled Robot )




Accelero-Botix is a new approach to the Human Machine Interface. It is based on implementation of acceleration sensor for robotics, graphics and embedded application development. This technology is now most widely used in virtual reality, mobile phones, gaming solutions, animated movies etc.
Control the robot using your hand gestures. Learn to sense your hand gestures with accelerometers and flex sensors and program the robot to do what you show!






2. LEGO BOT WORKSHOP





The LEGO Mind storm series of kits contain software and hardware to create small, customizable and programmable robots. They include a programmable 'Brick' computer that controls the system, a set of modular sensors and motors, and LEGO parts from the Technics line to create the mechanical systems.







3. ANDROID WORKSHOP





When people want to work parallel with the growing technology, learning the new technology is the primary step. To help all, This workshop which is based on Android operating system & its application development conceptualized by some top industry professionals. The main purpose is to teach students to be self-reliant and confident to build their own mobile application. This workshop will help them with understanding the Mobile software development ecosystem and opportunity to make their career in this booming Industry. The participants will be Officially Certified for their participation.






4. CYBER SECURITIES AND ETHICAL HACKING WORKSHOP





Ethical hacking is an important aspect in many national security agencies and corporate brands and is also a potential career opportunity. Hackers are assigned to prevent and curb any attempt to penetrate networks and/or computer systems using the same methods as a Hacker.
This workshop is specifically designed to provide the right knowledge to the aspiring Hacking and Information Security geeks. This workshop will explore various means and methods that an intruder exploits to gain access to computer resources.






5. CYBER CRIME INVESTIGATION AND CYBER FORENSICS WORKSHOP





We all know that the need for security certifications is on the rise, but how many of us have considered the alternate aspect of computer security: cyber-crime. Computer crime represents one of the fastest growing crime rates in the country and the need for computer forensics is growing

This workshop will explore the various means that an intruder has available to gain access to computer resources. It will investigate weaknesses by discussing the theoretical background behind, and whenever possible, actually performing the attack.
