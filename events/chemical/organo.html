ORGANO MODELS
This event basically will test the student concept and knowledge of organic chemistry isomers (R, S, dextro-rotary, Leavorotatory,cis-trans,etc). Students have to make the told organic molecules out of given ball and Sticks provided to them.

Eligibility: All students with a valid identity card of their respective educational institutes.

• Balls and sticks to be provided by the organisers
