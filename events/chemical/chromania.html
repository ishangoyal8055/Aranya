PROBLEM STATEMENT
In the first round contestants will be given a stained colored fabric which they will have to discolor and in the next round they will be provided with a white fabric which they will have to color in the most creative way with the help of chemicals.

Participation: 1 or 2 Members team
Eligibility: All students with a valid identity card of their respective educational institutes.
Rules:
• Event registration is free, once you are registered for Aranya.
• Any kind of cheating will lead to disqualification.
• You will be allowed to perform only once.
• All chemicals &equipments will be provided by us
• Any chemical brought by the participants will not be allowed to be used
• Chemicals will be provided once
• There will be two rounds, winners of 1st round will be eligible for 2nd round
• In the 1st round you will be given marks on the basis of maximum cleaning of fabric
• In the 2nd round you will be judged on the basis of maximum coloring of fabric
• Judge's decision will be last decision.
• We will not be responsible for any accidents during the event
The organizers can add, modify or remove the above mentioned rules without any prior notice. The changes would be informed to the participant
