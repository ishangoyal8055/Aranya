F FACTOR
HOW DOES WATER MOVES…?? WHEN IT MOVES WHAT HAPPEN..??
HOW DOES ITS FLOW VARY..??
HOW CAN WE INCREASE THE FLOW..??
These are the general questions prevailing in each of us mind. So this event guides us how to deal with these types of problems and prepares us for their solution.

In this very event, participant is supposed to make a flow of fluid from one side to the other side of a arena. We would be providing them the basic instruments like T-bents, U-bents, pipes and other necessities to generate the arena.

Participation: 1 or 2 Members team
Eligibility: All students with a valid identity card of their respective educational institutes.
Rules:
• Event registration is free, once you are registered for Aranya.
• Any kind of cheating will lead to disqualification.
• You will be allowed to perform only once.
• You have to work on the instruments which would be provided to you by us. No-one is supposed to bring their own instruments.
• Usage of pumps to increase the flow is strictly against the regulations. Anyone found using pumps for this very purpose would be disqualified immediately.
• Judge's decision will be last decision
• We will not be responsible for any accidents during the event
The organizers can add, modify or remove the above mentioned rules without any prior notice. The changes would be informed to the participants.
