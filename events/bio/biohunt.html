BIO HUNT
Bio-Hunt is a camp where every occupant is after the hidden treasure. Crack the puzzles, leave no stone unturned and reach 'The Treasure' in time. Act fast as you are not only the one around.
RULES:
• It is a team event with teams of two participants each
• Participants would be given the map of camp where lie the clues with a hidden treasure
• Participants will be given the exact location of the first entry point which is marked on the map
• First solution of the puzzle gives you the location of the next puzzle hidden
• The team, which is able to solve the final puzzle in the shortest time, will be the winner
