THE QUIZ
A quiz competition based on aptitude and computer/IT related knowledge. The event is a treat for all those geeks who really know their way around in the world of computers. Application of knowledge, logic, aptitude and the ability to think and analyze quickly will matter the most.
Rules and Specifications : 1. The event consists of two stages - Preliminary round and Final round.
2. The prelims will consist of 30 questions to be done on paper. There would questions based on the following topics: Aptitude/logical reasoning, Computer hardware/software knowhow, IT industry etc.
3. The duration for the prelims would be about 30 minutes.
4. The top four teams shall be selected for the Final Round.
5. The Final round would be conducted on the stage.
6. The Finals would have various rounds which would involve aptitude/logical reasoning, computer hardware/software knowhow etc.
7. Any team found using unfair means, damaging the hardware/software in any sort of way shall be disqualified.
8. The organizers can add, modify or remove the above mentioned rules without any prior notice.
Participation: 3 Member team.
Eligibility: All students with a valid identity card of their respective educational institutes and also their Aranya registration cards are eligible to participate.
FAQ-
• Is there any entry or participation fee?
No,the participation is absolutely free.
• Is this event only for Computer Science students?
No,the event is for students of all the branches and participation from other branches is most welcome
