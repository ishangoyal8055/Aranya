WEB DESIGNING EVENT
A team of two would be required to design a web page about a given topic in limited time. Web Development, creativity and Critical Thinking Skills will be tested. Rules and Specifications:
The participants will be provided with a php server, text editor(Notepad++/gEdit) .
1. An objective will be assigned to the teams and they will have to develop a web-based solution to it.
2. Teams will be given a set deadline to complete.
3. Marks shall be awarded on the basis of the degree to which the aforementioned objective has been achieved, creativity, design aesthetics, ease-of-use and innovation.
4. Any team found using unfair means, damaging the hardware/software in any sort of way shall be disqualified. The organizers can add, modify or remove the above mentioned rules without any prior notice.
Participation: 2 Member team.
Eligibility: All students with a valid identity card of their respective educational institutes and also their Aranya registration cards are eligible to participate.
FAQ-
• Is there any entry or participation fee?
No,the participation is absolutely free.
• Is this event only for Computer Science students?
No,the event is for students of all the branches and participation from other branches is most welcome
