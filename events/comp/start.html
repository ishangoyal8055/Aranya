START ME UP CHALLENGE
Teams will be presented some challenges/set of tasks which would include tweaking certain features/privileges etc.
Rules and Specifications:
1. This event will have two rounds – Prelims and Finals
2. The prelims will be written and teams will be given an hour to solve them.
3. Selected teams will go on to the final round – to be held in the lab(s).
4. Rest of the rules pertaining to the final round will be disclosed later.
Participation : 2 Member team
Eligibility: All students with a valid identity card of their respective educational institutes and also their Aranya registration cards are eligible to participate.
FAQ-
• Is there any entry or participation fee?
No,the participation is absolutely free.
• Is this event only for Computer Science students?
No,the event is open for students of all the branches and participation from other branches is most welcome
