PAPER PRESENTATION
A procedure in which the participants present the results of some scientific investigation or research. The oral presentation should use an effective way of communicating the research to the audience such as slides, graphs, etc. Technical Paper – is the one which gives details about your contribution to the field and the experiments performed.
Rules and specifications:
1. Papers to be presented during Aranya 2012 will be selected on the basis of the abstracts mailed online.
2. You can download the format for the abstract from the website. The short listed entries will have to present their paper at Thapar University during ARANYA 2012.
3. The abstract must be in .pdf or .doc format.
4. The prescribed format must be followed failing which may lead to disqualification of the participants.
5. Participants from different institutions can also form a team but all team members must be registered at ARANYA 2012 Website.
6. Participating teams should submit a hard copy to the coordinators at least 1 hour before the event.
7. The decision of the judges will be final and binding.
8. Online mailing of abstracts for paper presentations can be mailed up to 8th Nov. 2012.
Participation: A maximum of 2 students per team are allowed to present a paper (one as author and another as co-author). In case of team participation, both the members are required to be present on the spot of the event.
Eligibility: All students with a valid identity card of their respective educational institutes and also their Aranya registration cards are eligible to participate.

Please mail your entries ( Name & Summary/Complete technical paper ) to ahlawatvaibhav5@gmail.com by November 20th 20112.
